About me

spending almost two years in Data Science Research Center Shahrekord University as a research assistant and researcher was really helpful and caused me to become interested in teaching at the university so, I am a lecturer at Shahrekord University. Before that For a two-year period between 2017 and 2019, I held the post of research assistant in the Software Engineering Lab in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Islamic Azad University of Isfahan (Khorasgan) (IAU). I completed my M.Sc. in Software Engineering in January 2019.

I always enjoy doing research on Software Engineering for addressing problems in different domains, especially in Software Engineering, machine learning methods and interdisciplinary research in software engineering, AI and human aspects of software engineering.
